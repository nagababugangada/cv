About Prof Ramana Sonti:
I am currently a clinical Associate Professor in the Finance area. At ISB, I have taught various courses such as Corporate finance, Investment Analysis, Options & Futures, Advanced Portfolio Management, Value investing, Investing in private equity, Capital raising strategies in corporations, Econometrics, Theory of Finance etc. I have also taught at Tulane University and BITS PIlani -- Hyderabad campus.

My research interests include corporate governance, institutional investment behaviour, and links between financial and product markets. I have published research in leading journals such as Journal of Financial Economics, Review of Financial Studies, Journal of Financial Markets, The Accounting Review etc. My current interests are at the intersection of Economics and Deep Learning.
